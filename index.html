Our Legacy

A Tradition Born in Royal Kitchens

At Swad Bihar, we do not just cook; we preserve a legacy. Our recipes trace back to the royal kitchens of the Nawabs, where the art of Dum cooking was perfected over centuries.

Every handi of biriyani is sealed hermetically with dough (Dum process) and slow-cooked over wood charcoal embers. This allows the juices of marinated meats, saffron, and aromatic herbs to infuse perfectly into each individual grain of rice.

  • Pure, high-grade saffron imported from Kashmir
  • Hand-slaughtered Halal meats marinated for 12 hours
  • No artificial colors, preservatives, or MSG
